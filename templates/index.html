About Us

Fashion Wholesale
Done Right

Soho Apparel Group is a leading fashion wholesaler based in Southern California. We specialize in delivering trend-forward styles with uncompromising quality to retailers across the country.

Located at 15025 Proctor Avenue, City of Industry, our state-of-the-art facility supports rapid turnaround times, private labeling, and custom production — everything a modern retailer needs.
